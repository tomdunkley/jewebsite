FAQ (Frequently Anticipated Questions)

COVID queries

See our covid page for information about how we are dealing with the ongoing covid situation. You can also stay up to date by following our facebook and twitter. For further questions or clarification, email enquiries@juneevent.co.uk

Who can attend?

For reasons that I'm sure you can all understand, this year's event is college and alumni only. If you have any specific queries about how this affects your attendence, email enquiries@juneevent.co.uk and we'll be sure to get back to you ASAP.

I've changed my mind and can no longer attend. Can I get a refund?

Unfortunately, we cannot offer refunds except for exceptional circumstances, but we do offer name changes via fixr, as long as they are processed before 24/05/21. Selling tickets on is allowed but it is completely your responsibility. If doing this, bare in mind the above question about who can attend.

What's an exceptional circumstance?

We will offer refunds if:
- The event is cancelled
- You are unable to attend as you are self-isolating

We will consider further refund requests on a case-by-case basis. Email ticketing@juneevent.co.uk or enquiries@juneevent.co.uk for more information.
